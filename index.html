About Our Company

Trusted Engineers of Air Movement Since 1972

Industrial Fans India Private Limited (formerly Industrial Fans India) was established in 1972 and has built an outstanding reputation for engineering and building custom-made Air Movement & Control equipment. Every customer application demands unique design — we take pride in delivering exactly that.

Over five decades, we have supplied several hundred fan and damper systems to reputed customers in both private and public sector undertakings. Our ISO 9001:2008 quality system, technology partnerships with leading global names, and world-class manufacturing facility in Chennai set us apart.

Where We Serve

Industries We Power

From blast furnace air supply to flue gas desulphurisation — our equipment works in the most demanding process environments.

Power Stations

FD, ID, PA fans; boiler dampers; stack dampers for thermal and combined cycle plants.

Steel Plants

Series blowers for blast furnaces, circulation fans, hot blast stove dampers and diverter gates.

Cement Plants

Clinker cooler exhaust fans, kiln ID fans, raw mill fans and heavy-duty erosion-resistant impellers.

Paper Industries

DTPA valves, circulation fans and drying section air-handling equipment for paper mill processes.

Chemical Industries

Corrosion-resistant fans with ECTFE, rubber lining or FRP construction for aggressive media.

Waste Heat Recovery

Stack dampers, coffee pot dampers and high-temperature fans for WHR boiler systems.

FGD Plants

Booster fans and butterfly dampers with rubber lining for flue gas desulphurisation systems.

Process Industries

Custom fans for mining, petrochemical, pharmaceutical and general process plant applications.
